About Essence

Our story, mission, and commitment to quality

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Essence began with a simple mission: to create exceptional fragrances that tell a story and evoke emotion. Our founder, Elizabeth Chen, a perfumer with over 20 years of experience, believed that fragrance is more than just a scentâ€”it's a personal expression and a powerful memory maker.

What started as a small boutique in New York has grown into a beloved brand with customers worldwide. Despite our growth, we remain committed to our founding principles: quality ingredients, artisanal craftsmanship, and creating scents that become a part of life's most precious moments.

Our Process

How we craft our signature fragrances

1

Ingredient Selection

We source the finest ingredients from around the world, prioritizing quality, sustainability, and ethical sourcing practices.

2

Artisanal Blending

Our master perfumers carefully blend ingredients by hand, creating complex and harmonious scent profiles.

3

Maturation

Each fragrance is allowed to mature for the optimal time, allowing the notes to develop and blend perfectly.

4

Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes rigorous testing to ensure consistency, longevity, and the perfect scent profile.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through sustainable sourcing, eco-friendly packaging, and responsible manufacturing practices.

Cruelty-Free

All of our products are 100% cruelty-free. We never test on animals and work only with suppliers who share this commitment.

Community

We believe in giving back. A portion of every purchase supports organizations that empower women and protect the environment.
